## Iterm2 or Alacritty

Both are better terminal emulators then the built in mac terminal. Iterm2 is
full featured. Alacritty is new and blazing fast. [ITerm2](https://iterm2.com/)
[Alacritty](https://github.com/jwilm/alacritty)
